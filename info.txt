Connections:

    DHT11 Sensor to Arduino
        VCC → 5V
        GND → GND
        Data → D2

    L298N Motor Driver to Arduino
        IN1 → D3
        IN2 → D4
        GND → GND
        5V → 5V

    DC Motor to L298N
        Motor Wire 1 → OUT1
        Motor Wire 2 → OUT2

    Power (9V Battery)
        (+) to L298N 12V input
        (-) to GND (common ground with Arduino)
